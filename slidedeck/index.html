The future is Vocal!

A Cryptocurrency platform for promoting social change and civic engagement

Frustrations Felt:

  • Long been dominated by an older generation
  • The inability to communicate directly to government
  • No easy to use platform
  • No trackers for smaller communities or specific geographic areas
  • No untamperable records of an issue
  • Transparency

    • Vocal is a platform
    inspired by change.org
    but based on the immutability of the Blockchain with the advantages of GIS and Map based visualization.

    So what does Vocal solve ?

    Engagement in politics has reached record lows among younger demographics. We aim to provide a monetary benefit to improve engagement

    We have an immutable ledger of votes and issue creation (using the Neo blockchain) that serves as proof

    And a few more things...

  • A real incentive system for political participation, we created that system through the use of Vocal coin - which can be redeemed for agenda promotion, issue creation (and other things to come) through a traceable currency.
  • A widely accessible platform for political engagement and discovery
  • Yelp-like platform enables map-search of active issues. Quickly and easily find the issues that are most pertinent to you and your community, without having to sift through a world of political agendas/issues.
